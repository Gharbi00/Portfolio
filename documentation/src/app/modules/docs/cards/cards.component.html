Loyalty Card Settings

Introduction

The Loyalty Card Settings page in Elevok allows you to configure and customize the loyalty cards issued to new customers when they join your platform. This section is accessible in the Elevok Marketplace under the Customer Engagement app, specifically in the Cards tab.

Enabling Card Types

  • Physical Card: Enable the generation of physical loyalty cards.
  • Virtual Card: Enable the generation of virtual loyalty cards.
  • Both: Enable both physical and virtual loyalty cards for comprehensive coverage.

Card Generation

Loyalty cards will be automatically generated for each new customer upon joining the loyalty program.

Card Validity

Validity in Years: Specify the number of years the loyalty card will remain valid.

Visual Representation

Card Number Display: Choose how the card number will be displayed (e.g., numeric format, alphanumeric format).

Barcode/QR Code: Select the type of code to be displayed on the card.

  • Barcode: Choose the barcode algorithm (e.g., EAN, UPC).
  • QR Code: Opt for a QR code for modern scanning options.

Card Number Format

  • Numeric Only: Use numbers only for the card number.
  • Alphanumeric: Use a combination of letters and numbers for the card number.
  • Length of Card Number: Specify the desired length of the card number.

Card Design

Card Background Design: Upload a custom background image for each level to personalize the appearance of the loyalty cards.

Example Configuration

  • Card Type: Both physical and virtual cards enabled.
  • Validity: 3 years.
  • Visual Representation: Barcode displayed on the card.
  • Barcode Algorithm: EAN-13 for physical cards.
  • Card Number Format: Nnumeric, 10 characters long.
  • Background Design: Unique image uploaded for each loyalty level.

By configuring these settings, you can ensure that each loyalty card is tailored to your brand and meets your specific requirements, enhancing the overall customer experience. For more detailed guidance on configuring loyalty card settings, please refer to the Elevok Help Center.
