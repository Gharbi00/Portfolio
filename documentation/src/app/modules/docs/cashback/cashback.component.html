Cashback Settings

Introduction

The Cashback Settings page in Elevok allows you to configure cashback rewards for purchases made by your customers. You can find this section in the Elevok Marketplace under the Customer Engagement app, specifically in the Converter tab.

Configuring Cashback Rewards

Cashback rewards are defined as a percentage of the purchase amount, and you can reward customers with both quantitative points (monetary value) and qualitative points (status and levels value). These settings can be customized for different customer levels and platforms.

Cashback Percentages

  • Set the percentage of the purchase amount that will be rewarded as cashback.
  • Configure separate percentages for quantitative points (monetary rewards) and qualitative points (status and level rewards).

Customize for Different Levels

  • Specify different cashback percentages for each customer level you have created.
  • Ensure each level has its own unique reward structure to incentivize progression through the levels.

Customize for Different Platforms

Set different cashback percentages for each platform:

  • Physical Store: Define the cashback percentage for in-store purchases.
  • Webshop: Define the cashback percentage for online purchases made through your website.
  • Mobile Application: Define the cashback percentage for purchases made through your mobile app.

Example Configuration

Level 1:

  • Physical Store: 5% cashback in quantitative points, 2% in qualitative points.
  • Webshop: 4% cashback in quantitative points, 1.5% in qualitative points.
  • Mobile Application: 4.5% cashback in quantitative points, 1.8% in qualitative points.

Level 2:

  • Physical Store: 6% cashback in quantitative points, 2.5% in qualitative points.
  • Webshop: 5% cashback in quantitative points, 2% in qualitative points.
  • Mobile Application: 5.5% cashback in quantitative points, 2.2% in qualitative points.

Additional Settings

  • Percentage Adjustment: You can fine-tune the cashback percentages at any time to reflect changes in your marketing strategy or business goals.
  • Platform-Specific Customization: Ensure that each platform offers a competitive and attractive cashback percentage to drive engagement across all sales channels.

By configuring your cashback settings, you can effectively incentivize purchases and enhance customer loyalty. For more detailed guidance and advanced configurations, please refer to the Elevok Help Center.
