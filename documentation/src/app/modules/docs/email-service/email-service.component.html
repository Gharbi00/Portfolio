Email Services Configuration

Introduction

To send email notifications or marketing emails to your customers, you need to configure your email service settings. This section will guide you through the steps to set up your email service within Elevok. You can find these settings in the Elevok Marketplace under the Emails app.

Setting Up Email Server

  • Host: Enter the hostname of your email server.
  • User: Provide the username for your email account.
  • Port: Specify the port number your email server uses (e.g., 25, 465, 587).
  • Password: Enter the password for your email account.
  • Encryption: Choose the type of encryption for your email server (e.g., SSL, TLS).

Additional Settings

  • From Address: Set the default email address that will appear in the "From" field of your outgoing emails.
  • Reply-to Address: Specify the reply-to address if it differs from the from address.
  • Test Email: Send a test email to verify that your settings are correct.

Example Configuration

  • Host: smtp.your-email-provider.com
  • User: your-email@your-domain.com
  • Port: 587
  • Password: your-email-password
  • Encryption: TLS
  • From Address: no-reply@your-domain.com
  • Reply-to Address: support@your-domain.com

Sending Test Email

  • After entering all required information, use the "Send Test Email" button to ensure that your email settings are correctly configured.
  • Check your inbox for the test email to confirm receipt.

By properly configuring your email service, you can ensure that all notifications and marketing emails are reliably delivered to your customers, enhancing communication and engagement. For more detailed instructions and troubleshooting tips, please refer to the Elevok Help Center.
